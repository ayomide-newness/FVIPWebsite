About FVIP

Built on Trust,
Designed for Generations

Fish Valley Investments & Property Limited (FVIP) is the special purpose vehicle (SPV) wholly owned by Fish Valley Nigeria Limited (FVN), established to own, develop and manage the Fish Valley project and future real estate developments.

Our Story

A Decade of Trust
and Excellence

Fish Valley Investments & Property Limited (FVIP) was established with a clear purpose: to deliver real estate developments founded on integrity, transparency and disciplined execution.

Over the past decade, we have focused on building the right foundations before building the development itself. This has included securing and strengthening our land title, completing the legal and regulatory framework, establishing robust governance structures, and ensuring every stage of the project is supported by the appropriate approvals and documentation. We believe lasting developments begin with getting the fundamentals right.

Fish Valley is FVIP's flagship development: a 55-year sublease over six hectares delivered under a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) and Build-Operate-Transfer (BOT) arrangement with the Federal Radio Corporation of Nigeria (FRCN), located at 320 Adekunle Fajuyi Way, GRA Ikeja.

Every aspect of Fish Valley reflects our commitment to quality, sustainability and responsible development. From carefully planned neighbourhoods and vibrant mixed-use spaces to enduring infrastructure and community-focused amenities, we are creating more than a destination, we are delivering a place designed to serve generations.

Vision & Mission

Our Purpose

We exist to make real estate accessible, reliable, and genuinely rewarding for every client, from the first-time buyer to the institutional investor.

Fish Valley is more than a development. It is a bold statement of what urban living in Lagos can and should be. Rooted in security, functionality, and world-class infrastructure, Fish Valley represents the future of integrated communities in Ikeja: a place where families can live with peace of mind, professionals can thrive with modern conveniences, businesses can grow in synergy, and investors can anchor their capital in a resilient asset.

Our Vision is clear
To be the leading developer of sustainable, innovative and community-centric real estate developments that redefine modern living and create lasting value.
Our Mission is bold
To deliver exceptional real estate through thoughtful design, quality construction and sustainable development that creates lasting value for communities, businesses and investors.
