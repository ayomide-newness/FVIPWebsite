The Masterplan
Unveiled

A 6-hectare mixed-use development in Ikeja GRA; masterplanned across three phases with residential (max 6 floors), commercial and mixed-use zones (max 10 floors), an estate park, and a utility building, all underpinned by world-class infrastructure.
