Fish Valley Launches Phase II: Off-Plan Residential Units Now Open for Reservation
June 2026
4 min read

FVIP has announced the official launch of Phase II at Fish Valley, opening off-plan villas, apartments and town houses for reservation at pre-launch pricing with flexible payment plans.

Phase II builds directly on the infrastructure-first approach established in Phase I, extending serviced roads, drainage and utility corridors into the next stretch of the six-hectare masterplan. Early reservation is open to both individual buyers and diaspora investors, with documentation and site visits arranged through the Investor Portal.

Prospective buyers are encouraged to book a consultation to review unit types, pricing tiers and available payment structures before Phase II pricing adjusts at the next release stage.

Serviced Land Plots Now 65% Sold — Secure Yours Before Phase II Closes
May 2026
3 min read

Demand for serviced land within the Fish Valley masterplan continues to accelerate, with plots now 65% subscribed ahead of the Phase II close-out.

Each parcel is sold with full documentation and title support under the FRCN Public-Private Partnership, giving buyers a 55-year tenure backed by masterplan infrastructure — serviced roads, drainage, power and estate security — already under delivery.

Remaining plots are being allocated on a first-documented, first-secured basis. Interested investors are advised to speak with the sales team promptly to confirm availability and pricing.

📈 Insights
Why Ikeja GRA Continues to Outperform the Lagos Real Estate Market
April 2026
5 min read

Ikeja GRA remains one of Lagos's most resilient property corridors, combining established infrastructure, proximity to the business district, and a low supply of newly masterplanned mixed-use land.

Fish Valley sits inside this corridor, at 320 Adekunle Fajuyi Way, benefiting from existing road networks, utilities and institutional neighbours while introducing new-build residential, commercial and hospitality stock to a market that has seen little fresh, professionally documented supply.

For investors comparing locations across Lagos, the combination of established location value and new infrastructure is a key part of the Fish Valley investment case.
