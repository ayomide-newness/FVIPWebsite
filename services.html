FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the land tenure at Fish Valley?
Fish Valley sits on a 55-year sublease under a Public Private Partnership with the Federal Radio Corporation of Nigeria (FRCN). This provides long-term security and legal certainty for all investors and property owners within the estate.
What documentation comes with a property purchase?
Every property transaction is supported with a Deed of Assignment (D of A), purchase agreement, payment receipts, and all relevant regulatory documentation. Our legal team oversees and verifies every aspect of the process.
Can diaspora investors purchase properties?
Yes. We have a dedicated diaspora investment programme. Virtual site tours, remote documentation, and international payment arrangements are all supported. Many of our investors are Nigerians based in the UK, US, Canada, and Europe.
Are flexible payment plans available?
Yes. We offer flexible payment plans ranging from 6 to 24 months depending on the property type and agreed terms. Outright purchases attract preferential pricing. Payment plans are fully documented with no hidden fees.
Is there a property management service available?
Yes. FVIP offers a full property management service covering tenant sourcing, vetting, rent collection, maintenance management, and quarterly reporting - ideal for investors who want a fully managed, passive income asset.
How do I book a site visit?
You can book a site visit by contacting us via phone, WhatsApp (+234 911 741 5120), or email (info@fvipng.com). We schedule visits Monday to Saturday at your convenience and provide transport assistance from central Lagos.
Still have questions?

Our team is available Monday – Saturday to answer any queries.
